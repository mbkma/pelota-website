About Pelota

Pelota is a 3D tennis simulation game developed with passion using the Godot game engine. Our goal is to deliver an authentic tennis experience with realistic physics, intuitive controls, and engaging gameplay.

Whether you're a tennis enthusiast or a casual gamer, Pelota offers an immersive and enjoyable experience. The game combines accurate ball physics with responsive player mechanics, allowing for both competitive and casual play.
